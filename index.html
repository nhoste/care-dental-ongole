Dental Care Steps

Follow These Steps for a Healthy Smile
And Improved Oral Hygiene

Step1

Brush Your Teeth Twice a Day

  • Use fluoride toothpaste
  • Morning & Night

Regular brushing helps remove plaque and prevent tooth decay.

Step2

Floss Daily for Healthy Gums

  • Remove food particles
  • Before bedtime

Flossing helps clean areas that brushing alone cannot reach.

Step3

Use Mouthwash for Fresh Breath

  • Rinse with fluoride
  • Once daily

Mouthwash helps reduce bacteria and strengthens tooth enamel.

Step4

Visit Your Dentist Regularly

  • Professional cleaning
  • Every 6 months

Regular dental checkups help maintain oral health and catch issues early.
